Exercise Guide for Beginners

Start your fitness journey with these simple yet effective exercises.

1. Bodyweight Squat

A foundational movement that strengthens your legs, core, and glutes. Perfect to build lower body strength without equipment.

2. Push-Up (Knee Version)

Modified push-ups help beginners build upper body strength, targeting chest, shoulders, and triceps while protecting the lower back.

3. Plank Hold

Engage your core muscles with this classic exercise. Focus on maintaining a straight line from head to heels.

4. Glute Bridge

Strengthen your glutes and lower back. This simple movement is excellent for developing hip stability and reducing back pain.
